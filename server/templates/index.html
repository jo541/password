Generator of password

Welcome to generator of password with method Diceware . This method consist to roll 6 dices 6 times. The result of 6 dices set a nomber between 111111 and 666666. With result return a word . At the end with 6 word you have a phrase like "worldtipsdaterememberlikedo". It's more easier to remember password like this. It's possible to add a number and special charactere.By example replace 'e' by by 3 and 'a' by @ .
